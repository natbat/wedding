Lamb Kofte

Lamb minced with parsley, onions, herbs and mild spices, then grilled.

Marinated fish

Salmon and Cod marinated overnight in Nuri’s secret recipe and then baked.

Barbequed marinated chicken

Small portions of chicken marinated overnight then grilled on a charcoal barbeque.

Imam Bayilda

The translation of this dish is ‘Preachers and Baylida’. The story is that the Preacher fainted after tasting this wonderful dish. Aubergines halved and stuffed with rice, pine nuts, parsley and mixed herbs.

Biber Dolma

Turkish green peppers stuffed with rice, pine nuts, and mixed herbs.

Tomatoes Dolma

Tomatoes stuffed with rice, pine nuts and mixed herbs.

Taze Fasulye

Flat beans cooked in a special tomato sauce.

Fasulye Plaki

A traditional Turkish dish of beans baked in a special sauce.

Bulgar

Cracked brown wheat cooked in olive oil.

Coban Salatasi

The translation of this dish is “Shepherd Salad”. It contains spring onions, tomatoes, parsley, olive oil and lemon.

Cacik

Home-made yogurt mixed with parsley, cucumber, and coriander and garlic sauce.

Hummus and Turkish bread

Served to accompany the above dishes.

Baklava

This 900 year old Ottoman recipe is a hazelnut, pastry and honey sweet.

Mixed fruit

Selection of English cheeses

To be served after the ‘cutting of the cheese’ ceremony.
